# HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is a Power BI project designed to analyze employee data and provide actionable insights into workforce trends, employee attrition, job satisfaction, and overall organizational performance.

The dashboard helps HR teams make data-driven decisions by identifying key factors affecting employee retention and workforce productivity.

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Monitor workforce demographics.
* Evaluate employee satisfaction levels.
* Identify departments with high attrition rates.
* Support strategic HR decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes employee-related information such as:

* Employee ID
* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Education
* Monthly Income
* Job Satisfaction
* Years at Company
* Attrition Status

## Dashboard Features

### 1. Employee Overview

* Total Employees
* Active Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate

### 2. Attrition Analysis

* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Job Role
* Attrition by Age Group
* Attrition by Gender

### 3. Employee Demographics

* Age Distribution
* Education Distribution
* Gender Distribution

### 4. Salary Analysis

* Average Monthly Income
* Salary Distribution by Department
* Salary Comparison Across Job Roles

### 5. Job Satisfaction Analysis

* Satisfaction Levels
* Department-wise Satisfaction Trends

## Key Insights

* Certain departments experience higher attrition rates than others.
* Younger employees show a higher tendency to leave the organization.
* Job satisfaction has a significant impact on employee retention.
* Income and years of experience influence attrition patterns.
